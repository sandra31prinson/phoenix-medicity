About Us

A BEACON OF SERVICE & PROFESSIONALISM

The rural locale of chettuwa, in the south Western Coastal Shore of Kerala is a pride for the Thrissur District with its exquisitely well – set natural serenity, weather, back waters and all the more people who have a willingness to strive, struggle, persevere and achieve.
It was in 1973 that a wealthy and large hearted NRI businessman and native of chettuva chose to set up a hospital here. The hospital was inaugurated by Dilip Kumar, a well-known and respected doyen Of Indian celluloid world. Over the years, T.M. hospital has turned out to be A synonym for chettuwa’s enduring pride and achievement.
After over two decades, in 1995, when the hospital was finding its existence almost in doldrums Dr. E.K. Ramachandran specialised in Paediatrics, plunged forth to take up the reins of the hospital. Dr. M. Sujatha his life partner and a qualified Gynaecologist also joined in this venture.
