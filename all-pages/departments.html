LASER SURGERIES
Laser for Piles

Laser treatment for piles is a minimally invasive procedure that uses a laser to shrink and remove hemorrhoids. It is a safe and effective treatment with a quick recovery time and minimal discomfort. It is performed by experienced doctors using advanced technology to ensure the best possible outcome.

Laser for Fistula

Laser treatment for fistula is a minimally invasive procedure that uses a laser to remove the infected tract and promote healing. The laser seals the tract and destroys the infected tissue, which reduces the risk of recurrence and eliminates the need for major surgery. Patients can expect a quick recovery time, and the procedure has a high success rate with minimal risk of complications.

Laser for Fissures

Laser treatment for fissure is a minimally invasive procedure that uses a laser to repair the anal fissure. The laser seals the nerve endings & blood vessels around the fissure, promoting healing and relieving the pain and discomfort associated with the condition. Laser treatment for fissure is an excellent option for those who want a minimally invasive and painless solution for this common condition.

Laser for Pilonidal Sinus

Laser treatment for pilonidal sinus is a minimally invasive and effective procedure that uses a laser to remove the infected tissue and promote healing. The laser seals the tract and eliminates the infected tissue, which reduces the risk of recurrence and eliminates the need for major surgery. The procedure has a high success rate and reduces the chances of complications associated with traditional surgery.
